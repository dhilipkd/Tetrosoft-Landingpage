TetroONE

TetroONE is a unified ERP suite that integrates core business functions into a single AI-driven platform, helping organizations operate efficiently and adapt quickly to change.From automated financial management and smart HR operations to intelligent supply chain control, TetroONE simplifies complex processes and empowers leaders with actionable insights.

TetroPOS

TetroPOS is a cloud-based retail management system that combines billing, inventory, and analytics to help businesses run efficiently and scale with ease.From real-time stock tracking to automated invoicing and powerful sales insights, TetroPOS simplifies operations so you can focus on growth, customers, and profitability.

TetroPAY

TetroPAY unifies recruitment, onboarding, employee, and asset management into one intelligent workforce platform.With AI-driven attendance, leave & shift management, and automated payroll, it ensures accuracy, efficiency, and compliance.TetroPAY delivers timely salaries, tax calculations, and statutory reporting—so businesses can focus on growth.

Assetra

Assetra provides end-to-end asset lifecycle management, from procurement to retirement, with real-time monitoring to improve productivity and reduce costs. Automated maintenance, smart alerts, and analytics help maximize asset utilization and support data-driven decisions. With an intuitive dashboard and clear reports, Assetra ensures full visibility, proactive planning, and minimal downtime.

TexsuitePro

TexSuitePro is an all-in-one software for the garments and textile industry, streamlining inventory, production, quality control, and delivery. With real-time insights, automation, customizable dashboards, and detailed analytics, it helps manage multiple production lines, track raw materials, optimize workflows, and ensure timely, profitable growth.
